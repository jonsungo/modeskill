# Decision Taxonomy

Assign exactly one category:

- `inherited`: target follows the reference intent without material product or UX change.
- `adapted`: target preserves reference intent with a justified local adjustment.
- `project-specific`: a justified target-only decision not expected to propagate.
- `unresolved`: evidence is missing, conflicting, inaccessible, or too weak.
- `deprecated`: evidence shows a pattern is obsolete, replaced, or explicitly legacy.

Consistency does not require visually identical output. Brand, density, workflow, and domain differences may be `adapted` or `project-specific` when evidence supports them.
